3. Data We Collect

We collect different types of personal data depending on how you use DMsgBox. All data collection is necessary for providing our services.

3.1 Data You Provide Directly

  • Email Address: Collected during account creation for authentication and password recovery
  • Password: Securely encrypted and stored locally on your device
  • Messages: Your encrypted messages (stored for 24 hours then auto-deleted)
  • Profile Information: Optional display name or avatar you choose to add

3.2 Data Collected Automatically

  • Device Identifiers: Unique device ID and device name (for device revocation system)
  • Device Information: Android version, device model (used for compatibility)
  • Activity Logs: Login/logout times, message send times (automatically deleted after 90 days)
  • Biometric Data: Fingerprint or facial recognition (stored locally on your device only, never transmitted)

3.3 Data NOT Collected

We do NOT collect or process:

  • Location data
  • Contacts list
  • Camera or microphone recordings
  • Analytics or usage statistics
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Cookies or tracking data

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your personal data on one or more of the following legal bases:

4.1 Consent

For any non-essential processing, we obtain your explicit consent before collecting data. You can withdraw consent at any time through Settings → Privacy & Data → Manage Consent.

4.2 Contract

Processing is necessary to perform our contract with you (deliver encrypted messages, authenticate your account, manage your devices).

4.3 Legal Obligation

We may process data if required by law (e.g., responding to valid legal orders from authorities).

4.4 Legitimate Interest

We process data for security, fraud prevention, and system administration purposes. Your privacy rights are balanced with our legitimate interests.

5. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data only for the purposes described below:

  • Message Delivery: To send and receive encrypted messages
  • Authentication: To verify your identity and secure your account
  • Account Management: To manage your profile, settings, and preferences
  • Device Management: To track and revoke device access for security
  • Security: To detect and prevent unauthorized access or fraud
  • Service Improvement: To fix bugs and improve app functionality (anonymized only)
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • Two-Factor Authentication: To generate and validate TOTP codes

We do NOT use your data for:

  • Marketing or advertising purposes
  • Selling or sharing with third parties (except as noted below)
  • Building user profiles for behavioral analysis
  • Automated decision-making or profiling

6. Data Retention and Automatic Deletion

This is critical to privacy: We delete data automatically after specific periods.

🗑️ Automatic Data Deletion Schedule:

  • Messages: Automatically deleted 24 hours after creation (non-recoverable)
  • Activity Logs: Automatically deleted after 90 days
  • Email Address: Deleted when account is deleted
  • Device Identifiers: Deleted when device is revoked or account deleted
  • Biometric Data: Never sent to servers, only stored locally on your device

6.1 Account Deletion

When you request account deletion through Settings → Privacy & Data → Delete Account:

  1. Deletion is scheduled for 24 hours from request
  2. A confirmation email is sent to your registered address
  3. You can cancel deletion by clicking the link in the confirmation email (within 24 hours)
  4. After 24 hours, all your data is permanently deleted and cannot be recovered

6.2 Backups

Automated backups of your deleted data are also purged within 30 days of deletion. We retain audit logs of the deletion (encrypted) for 1 year for compliance purposes only.

7. Data Sharing and Processors

7.1 Who We Share Data With

We share your data only with:

Supabase (Data Processor)

  • Data Shared: Email, encrypted messages, device IDs, activity logs
  • Purpose: Backend database, authentication, message storage
  • Data Processing Agreement: In place
  • Location: Supabase infrastructure (see Supabase Privacy Policy)
  • Encryption: All data encrypted end-to-end before transmission

Stripe (Payment Processor - KIV)

  • Status: Currently not integrated (planned for future paid features)
  • If Integrated: Only payment information (credit card, billing address)
  • Non-sharing: Stripe will NOT have access to messages, emails, or device info

7.2 Who We DO NOT Share Data With

  • Marketing or advertising companies
  • Data brokers or analytics platforms
  • Social media companies
  • Government agencies (unless legally required with valid warrant)
  • Any other third parties without your explicit consent

7.3 Government Requests

We may disclose your data only when legally required by valid government order (warrant, subpoena) after exhausting legal remedies. We will notify you of such requests unless legally prohibited.

8. Your Rights (GDPR & PDPA)

Depending on your location, you have the following rights regarding your personal data. To exercise any right, email us at DMsgBox@alsocando.com:

8.1 Right to Access (Article 15 GDPR / Section 13 PDPA)

You have the right to request a copy of all personal data we hold about you. You can download your data at any time through:

Settings → Privacy & Data → Download My Data

Your data will be provided in JSON format within 5 business days.

8.2 Right to Erasure / Deletion (Article 17 GDPR / Section 15 PDPA)

You have the right to have your personal data deleted. You can request deletion through:

Settings → Privacy & Data → Delete Account

Your account and all associated data will be deleted within 24 hours.

8.3 Right to Rectification (Article 16 GDPR / Section 14 PDPA)

You can update your email address at any time through Settings → Account → Change Email.

8.4 Right to Data Portability (Article 20 GDPR)

You can export your data in a machine-readable format (JSON) through:

Settings → Privacy & Data → Download My Data

8.5 Right to Restrict Processing (Article 18 GDPR / PDPA)

You can restrict processing of your data at any time through:

Settings → Privacy & Data → Manage Consent

8.6 Right to Withdraw Consent (GDPR & PDPA)

If we process data based on your consent, you can withdraw it anytime through:

Settings → Privacy & Data → Manage Consent

8.7 Right to Lodge a Complaint (GDPR & PDPA)

If you believe your rights are violated, you have the right to lodge a complaint with:

  • GDPR (EU/UK): Your local Data Protection Authority
  • PDPA (Singapore): Personal Data Protection Commission (PDPC) - www.pdpc.gov.sg

9. International Data Transfers

9.1 Supabase (Backend)

Supabase may store your data in locations outside your country. For EU/UK users, this may involve data transfers to the US. We rely on Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) as approved by the European Commission.

9.2 Singapore Users (PDPA Compliance)

For Singapore users, data transfers are only to countries with adequate protection levels. We ensure compliance with Singapore PDPA's cross-border data transfer rules.

10. Data Security

We implement multiple layers of security to protect your data:

10.1 Encryption

  • At Rest: SQLCipher AES-256 encryption for all stored data
  • In Transit: HTTPS with TLS 1.2+ for all API calls
  • End-to-End: Messages encrypted on your device before transmission
  • Biometric: Fingerprint/Face data never transmitted, stored locally only

10.2 Access Controls

  • Password protection for account access
  • Biometric authentication (fingerprint/face)
  • Two-factor authentication (TOTP)
  • Device revocation system for compromised devices

10.3 No Logging of Sensitive Data

We do NOT log message content, email addresses, or other sensitive data. Any crash logs are filtered to remove PII before storage.

10.4 Limited Scope

Our backend has minimal access. It cannot:

  • Decrypt your messages
  • Access your biometric data
  • View your TOTP secrets
  • Know your password

Our incident response team will:

  • Immediately isolate affected systems
